Indian Traditional Recipe

One of the traditional drinks in India is called "lassi." Lassi is a popular beverage that originated in the Punjab region of Northern India but is now enjoyed throughout the country. It is a refreshing and cooling drink, particularly popular during the hot summer months.

Lassi is commonly served in tall glasses and can be enjoyed as a standalone drink or alongside a meal. It is known for its cooling properties and is believed to aid digestion. Lassi is a versatile beverage that caters to various tastes, whether you prefer it plain, sweet, fruity, or spiced.
